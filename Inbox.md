# Current Learning Philosophy

Do not learn technologies for the sake of learning technologies.

Always understand:

* Why does this exist?
* What problem does it solve?
* How does it work internally?
* What are the tradeoffs?

Examples:

* Why object files exist
* Why linking exists
* Why C is used for operating systems
* Why CPUs execute instructions the way they do
* Why databases are faster than plain files
* Why access and refresh tokens both exist

Understanding lasts longer than memorization.
